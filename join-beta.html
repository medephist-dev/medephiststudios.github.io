Join the Tyrian Vault Beta

Help shape the app before its public release on Google Play.

Two quick steps. Because Tyrian Vault is in closed testing, Google requires testers to join a group before they can install. Use the same Google account on both steps and on your phone's Play Store, or the download link won't appear.

1

Join the testers group

Join the Google Group so your account is approved as a tester. It's free and you can leave any time.

Join the testers group →
2

Opt in & get it on Google Play

Once you've joined the group, open the testing link and tap "Become a tester", then "Download it on Google Play" to install.

Become a tester on Google Play →

Not seeing the download? Make sure you joined the group first and that your phone's Play Store is signed in to the same Google account. It can take a few minutes for Google to register a new tester. Hit a bug or have feedback? Send a bug report or contact support — it genuinely helps.
