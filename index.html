Signature Features

Built around the things other apps can't do

Two heavyweight features anchor Tyrian Vault. Everything else orbits around them.

🔔 Exclusive

Real Notifications & Alarms

The feature no other GW2 app has. Set an alarm for any world boss or event and get a true OS-level push notification with sound — even when the app is closed and your screen is off.

These aren't in-app countdowns. They're real system alarms that wake your device. Set it before a long farm session and put your phone down — we'll tell you when it's time.

🧮 Smart Crafting

Pricing Calculator & Crafting Assistant

Search any craftable item and instantly compare its Trading Post price against the cost to craft it yourself — broken down material by material.

The Can Craft view shows every item you can make right now from materials you already have. No tab-switching, no manual counting, no spreadsheets.

Your data stays yours

Tyrian Vault connects using your own personal GW2 API key — generated by you, stored only on your device. We never see it, store it, or share it. No account required on our end.
