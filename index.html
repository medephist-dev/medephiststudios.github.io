What's Inside

Everything you need in Tyria

🔔

Real Notifications & Alarms

The feature no other GW2 app has. Set an alarm for any world boss or event and get a push notification with sound — even when the app is closed and your screen is off. Put your phone down. We'll tell you when it's time.

⏱️

World Boss Timers

Live countdowns for every world boss across all maps. Never miss Tequatl, Triple Trouble, or the Shatterer again.

⚒️

Crafting Assistant

See exactly what materials you're missing. Checks your inventory, bank, and all character bags automatically — no manual counting.

🏆

Legendary Crafting Tracker

Track your legendary progress, see what you still need, and compare make-vs-buy costs with live Trading Post prices.

👤

Account & Characters

View your characters, equipment, wallet, currencies, and inventory at a glance. Supports multiple GW2 accounts.

🗺️

Interactive Maps

Points of interest, waypoints, boss spawn locations, and mastery insights — for every zone in Tyria.

⚔️

WvW, PvP & Guilds

Live WvW objective maps with team ownership and tier badges. PvP stats and leaderboards. Guild roster, storage, and missions.

📅

Daily Reset & Wizard's Vault

Track your daily and weekly objectives, Wizard's Vault progress, and reset timers — all in one place.

💰

Trading Post

Check live item prices, track your listings, and find the best deals without launching the game.

🔒 Your data stays yours

Tyrian Vault connects using your own personal GW2 API key — generated by you, stored only on your device. We never see it, store it, or share it. No account required on our end.
