One developer, no shortcuts

Medephist Studios is an independent studio run by a single developer. Every app starts the same way: something we wanted to exist, built until it's good enough that we use it every day ourselves.

That means no ads, no data harvesting, and no feature held hostage behind a paywall. It also means when you email support, the person who answers is the person who wrote the code.
