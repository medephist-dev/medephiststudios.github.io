Tyrian Vault

The unofficial Guild Wars 2 companion built by a player, for players. Free. No ads. No paywalls.

Get it on Google Play ♥ Support Development

Free forever · Android · Requires a GW2 account

What's Inside

Everything you need in Tyria

⏱️

World Boss Timers

Real-time countdowns for every world boss. Set alarms so you never miss Tequatl or Triple Trouble again.

⚒️

Crafting Assistant

See exactly what materials you're missing. Checks your inventory, bank, and all character bags automatically.

👤

Account & Characters

View your characters, equipment, currencies, and inventory at a glance. Supports multiple accounts.

🗺️

Map & POI Tools

Track points of interest, waypoints, and zone completion across all of Tyria.

🔔

Notifications & Alarms

Get alerted for boss spawns and events. Works in the background on Samsung and all Android devices.

💰

Trading Post

Check item prices, track your listings, and find the best deals without launching the game.

🔒 Your data stays yours

Tyrian Vault connects using your own personal GW2 API key — generated by you, stored only on your device. We never see it, store it, or share it. No account required on our end.
